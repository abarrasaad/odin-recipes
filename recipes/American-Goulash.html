American Goulash

Exploring the Comforting Delight of American Goulash

American Goulash is a hearty and comforting one-pot dish that combines ground meat, typically beef, with macaroni noodles, tomatoes, and a medley of flavorful herbs and spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper, or to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Heat oil in a pot over medium-high heat.
  3. Cook, stirring occasionally, until flavors come together, about 3 minutes.
  4. Increase the heat to medium-high and bring to a rapid simmer.
  5. Remove from the heat and discard bay leaves. Stir in Cheddar and parsley.
