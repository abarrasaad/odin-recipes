Lasagna

A Symphony of Flavors in Classic Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that features multiple layers of flat pasta sheets, typically alternating with rich and flavorful fillings. The layers are commonly separated by a combination of ingredients such as seasoned ground meat (usually beef or pork), tomato sauce, ricotta or b√©chamel sauce, and melted cheese, creating a delicious and hearty casserole.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Cheeses

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
